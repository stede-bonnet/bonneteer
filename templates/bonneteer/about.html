How Bonneteer Works

From it's database of trusted sites (1), bonneteer sends out requests looking for trusted sources (2). Returning the page on which they appear on

Getting trusted sites

Bonneteer updates its database of trusted sites weekly by getting the megathread from the r/PiratedGames sub, parsing its content and spitting out a list of trusted releases and websites

Getting trusted repackers

With the list of trusted releases and safe sites, bonneteer requests all sites by threading, and returning the page where the release was found, future versions will include direct links

The name

Stede bonnet was an early eighteenth-century Barbadian pirate, sometimes called "The Gentleman Pirate" because he was a moderately wealthy land-owner before turning to a life of crime. Most people pirating games can probably buy them, but why do that when you can pirate them, the Bonnet way!
